# Market Comparison for the Software Administrative Support Role

This document summarizes market data for roles comparable to the "Software Administrative Support" position at White Pine Renovation. The analysis covers the local West Michigan market (Kalamazoo/Grand Rapids) and the remote job market.

**White Pine Stated Pay Scale:**
*   **Hourly:** $20.00 - $27.50
*   **Annualized:** ~$41,600 - $57,200

---

## 1. Local Market Analysis (Kalamazoo & Grand Rapids, MI)

The local market data suggests the White Pine role is paid appropriately for a "Specialist" title but underpaid for an "Analyst" title.

| Job Title | Average Pay (Kalamazoo/Grand Rapids) | Comparison to White Pine Offer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sales Support Specialist** | ~$24.00 / hour | The White Pine range is consistent with this title. |
| **Marketing Specialist** | ~$53,000 / year (~$25.50/hr) | The White Pine range is consistent with this title. |
| **Sales Operations Analyst** | ~$34.50 / hour | The White Pine offer is **significantly below** market rate for this title. |

**Conclusion:** Locally, the role is compensated as a support or specialist position, not an analyst position, despite having some analyst-level responsibilities (e.g., process improvement, reporting).

---

## 2. Remote Market Analysis

The remote market shows a clear distinction between support-level roles and more strategic analyst roles.

| Job Title | Average Pay (Remote, USA) | Comparison to White Pine Offer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Remote IT Support Specialist** | ~$24.70 / hour | The White Pine range is **directly in line** with the average for this role. |
| **Remote Business Systems Analyst** | ~$49.50 / hour | The White Pine offer is **~50% below** the market rate for this title. |

**Conclusion:** In the broader remote market, the compensation aligns almost perfectly with an IT Support Specialist role. It does not align with the higher-skilled (and higher-paid) Business Systems Analyst role.

---

## 3. Overall Analysis & Key Takeaways

1.  **Title vs. Responsibility Mismatch:** The responsibilities of the White Pine job (CRM optimization, reporting, process improvement) overlap with those of a "Sales Operations Analyst" or "Business Systems Analyst." However, the pay is benchmarked against a "Support Specialist" or "Coordinator" role.
2.  **Entry-Level Alignment:** As an entry-level position in the field, the pay is consistent with other support-focused roles, both locally and remotely. The company is not paying for prior expertise in operations analysis.
3.  **Negotiation Leverage:** Your negotiation leverage is strongest when arguing for the **top end of their stated range** ($27.50/hr). Arguing for a salary significantly above their range will likely be unsuccessful, as the pay is clearly benchmarked at the support/specialist level. Your argument should be based on the value your technical skills bring to the "analyst" and "developer" aspects of the job.
4.  **The "Real" Offer:** The true value of this job is the wide range of experience offered, which is rare for a role at this pay grade. The compensation for your first 1-2 years is not just the salary, but the skills you will acquire to command a market-rate salary in your next position.
