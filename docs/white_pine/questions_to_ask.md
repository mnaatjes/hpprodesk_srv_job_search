# Strategic Questions to Ask at the White Pine Interview


### Category 1: Questions About the Role & Day-to-Day Responsibilities

1.  **"What are the biggest workflow challenges you are want soled in the first six months?"**

2.  **"Could you walk me through a typical day or week in this role (i.e. tasks spread from admin to marketing et alia)?"**

3.  **"The job description mentions managing in-house financing programs. Could you tell me more about the day-to-day administration of that process and what software is currently used?"**

### Category 2: Questions About the Team & Company Culture

1.  **"What does the training and onboarding process look like for this role?"**

2.  **"I noticed the company strategy of 'Lower Overhead Means More of Your Money.' Does this mean you have a lean operation?"**

3.  **"How long have you been here? Do you like it? What do you like about it specifically?"**

### Category 3: Questions About Success & Future Growth

1.  **"What does success look like for this role in the first 90 days, and how would my performance be measured?"**

2.  **"The job description mentions 'opportunities to grow your technical and administrative skills.' I'm excited about this. Can you describe in more detail?"**
