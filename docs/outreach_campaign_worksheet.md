# Outreach Campaign Worksheet

This document contains the pre-filled email templates for your first outreach campaign. 

**Your Action Items:**
1.  For each target, spend 3-5 minutes on their website or blog.
2.  Find one specific, genuine detail to use for the `[Custom Hook]`.
3.  Replace the `[Custom Hook]` placeholder in the email body.
4.  Copy the subject and body and send the email from your professional email address.
5.  Update the company's status in your Notion board to "Outreach Sent" and set a follow-up date.
