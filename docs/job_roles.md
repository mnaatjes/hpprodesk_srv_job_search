## Job Roles & Descriptions

This document outlines potential job roles, their descriptions, and where to find them, tailored to your skillset.

---

### PHP / Backend Developer

*   **Core Description:** Responsible for server-side web application logic and integration of the work front-end developers do. They are usually responsible for writing the web services and APIs used by front-end developers and mobile application developers. This role focuses on databases, scripting, and the architecture of websites.

*   **Why You're a Good Fit:** This is your strongest area. Your `abac-auth` and `registry` projects are high-quality, object-oriented libraries that demonstrate a deep understanding of modern PHP, testing, and software design principles. This is what companies look for in a strong backend candidate.

*   **Keywords for Searching:** `PHP Developer`, `Backend Engineer`, `Laravel Developer`, `Symfony Developer`, `Server-side Engineer`.

*   **Where to Find This Role:**
    *   **Full-Time / Remote (High Quality):** `LaraJobs`, `Stack Overflow Jobs`, `We Work Remotely`.
    *   **Full-Time / Remote (High Volume):** `LinkedIn`, `Indeed`, `Glassdoor`.
    *   **Part-Time / Contract:** `Toptal`, `Upwork`.

---

### DevOps Engineer

*   **Core Description:** A role that bridges the gap between software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops). The goal is to shorten the development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality. This involves automation, building infrastructure, and maintaining development/production environments.

*   **Why You're a Good Fit:** Your extensive work with Docker, creating custom development environments with `docker-compose.yml`, and building automation scripts like your `docker-scaffold` project are direct examples of DevOps work. You have a strong foundation in developer tooling.

*   **Keywords for Searching:** `DevOps Engineer`, `Platform Engineer`, `SRE`, `Docker`, `CI/CD`, `AWS`, `Infrastructure`.

*   **Where to Find This Role:**
    *   **Full-Time / Remote:** `LinkedIn`, `We Work Remotely`, `Stack Overflow Jobs`. Look for companies that also list PHP in their stack.
    *   **Part-Time / Contract:** `Toptal`, `Upwork`. Many companies need short-term help to containerize their applications or set up deployment pipelines.

---

### Full-Stack Developer (Backend-Focused)

*   **Core Description:** A developer who is comfortable working on both the frontend (client-side) and backend (server-side) of an application. In your case, this would be a role where the primary focus is on the backend, but you are expected to contribute to the frontend as well.

*   **Why You're a Good Fit:** Your backend skills are professional-grade. Your GitHub repositories show you have an interest in and have experimented with frontend technologies like Vue.js and Alpine.js, making you a versatile candidate for teams that need developers who can work across the entire application.

*   **Keywords for Searching:** `Full-Stack PHP Developer`, `PHP Vue Developer`, `Laravel Full Stack`, `Full Stack Engineer`.

*   **Where to Find This Role:**
    *   **Full-Time / Remote:** `AngelList / Wellfound` (great for startups), `LinkedIn`, `We Work Remotely`.
    *   **Part-Time / Contract:** `Upwork`.
