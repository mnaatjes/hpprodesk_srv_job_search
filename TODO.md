# Final Interview Preparation Checklist

This is your prioritized to-do list for the time leading up to your interview at 1:00 PM tomorrow.

---

### **Priority 1: Rehearse Your Narrative (Tonight)**

The goal is to internalize your story so it comes out naturally, not memorized.

- [ ] **Practice Aloud:** Read your tailored "Professional Summary" from the `interviewer_summary.md` document out loud several times. This is your answer to "Tell me about yourself."
- [ ] **Talk Through Your Stories:** Pick your top 3 STAR stories from the `interview_prep_plan.md` (e.g., WordPress site, CRM process idea, troubleshooting). Tell these stories out loud, from start to finish, as if you were speaking to the interviewer. Don't worry about getting the words perfect; focus on the key points.
- [ ] **Rehearse Key Questions:** Practice your answers for the most critical questions from the `interview_prep_plan.md`, especially:
    - Your WordPress experience (mention `mnaatjes.com` and WooCommerce).
    - Your lack of direct CRM experience (focus on your ability to learn and map workflows).
    - Your troubleshooting process.

### **Priority 2: Review Key Materials (Tomorrow Morning)**

This is a quick final review to refresh your memory. Do not cram.

- [ ] **Final Read-Through:** Re-read the `interviewer_summary.md` document one last time. This is your "cheat sheet" and contains the most important narrative points.
- [ ] **Review Your Questions:** Look over the list of questions *you* prepared to ask *them*. Having thoughtful questions ready is crucial.
- [ ] **Glance at the Job Description:** Briefly re-read the "Key Responsibilities" to have them fresh in your mind.

### **Priority 3: Logistics & Setup (Tonight / Tomorrow Morning)**

Handle the logistics so you are not stressed before the interview.

- [ ] **Confirm Details:** Double-check the interview time (1:00 PM), exact address in Portage, MI, and the names of who you'll be meeting with.
- [ ] **Plan Your Route:** Check traffic and plan to arrive 10-15 minutes early.
- [ ] **Prepare Your Attire:** Choose and lay out a business-casual outfit.
- [ ] **Prepare Your Documents:** Print 3 copies of your resume and 3 copies of the `interviewer_summary.md`. Place them in a professional folder with a notepad and a pen.

### **Priority 4: Final Mindset (Tonight & Tomorrow)**

- [ ] **Do One Final Search:** Take 10 minutes to search "White Pine Renovation news" to ensure you haven't missed anything recent.
- [ ] **Stop Preparing:** Set a cut-off time tonight (e.g., 9 PM) and stop actively preparing. Your brain needs time to consolidate the information.
- [ ] **Get a Good Night's Sleep:** This is one of the most effective forms of preparation you can do now.
- [ ] **Relax:** On the day of the interview, have a good meal, listen to some music, and walk in with confidence. You are well-prepared.
